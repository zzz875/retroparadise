Delorean DMC-12
1981 - 1983

Did you know that the legendary DeLorean DMC-12 almost never saw the light of day? The DeLorean Motor Company was founded by former GM vice president John DeLorean, who dreamed of creating a revolutionary supercar. But things didn't go as planned... Scandal, bankruptcy, and arrest! In 1982, John DeLorean was accused of drug smuggling, allegedly to save his company from bankruptcy. Although he was later acquitted, production of the DMC-12 was stopped, and only around 9,000 units were ever made. But here's the strangest part: some of these cars are still sealed in containers, waiting for their moment. Maybe one of them could become your time machine?
