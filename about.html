About us

At RetroParadise, we believe there's a deeper, more inspiring way to connect with the past. Not just through nostalgia, but through exploration, restoration, and reimagination. We're passionate about timeless design, enduring technology, and the cultural spirit of the 80s and 90s. Our mission is to celebrate this golden era by bringing it back to life — through curated content, community stories, and guides that make retro cars, games, and gadgets accessible to everyone. In a world that’s always rushing forward, we help people rediscover the beauty of slowing down and looking back.

Our Founding

RetroCore was founded by Zaur Bashirov out of a deep love for the aesthetics and spirit of a past era — a time when technology had personality and design told a story. It began as a small passion project: just a blog and a few photo collections, curated by enthusiasts who spent hours restoring retro consoles, collecting rare car brochures, and digging into forgotten tech. What started as a place to share memories quickly grew into a vibrant community. Along the way, we began writing guides, building archives, and connecting with others who felt the same spark. RetroCore became more than just a website — it became a home for those who believe the past still has something important to say.
